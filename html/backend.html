Now we tell you which are the skills that we are looking for:

We are seeking a highly skilled Artificial Intelligence Specialist to join our team. The ideal candidate should have a Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence, with demonstrable experience in natural language processing and machine learning. Additionally, they should have advanced programming skills and experience deploying AI solutions in virtual environments.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop natural language processing and machine learning models to address specific business challenges.
  • Design and implement machine learning algorithms that are efficient and scalable.
  • Conduct data analysis and generate reports on the results obtained.
  • Code and implement AI solutions in virtual environments.
  • Participate in technical decision-making for the AI team.
  • Collaborate with other teams in the company to integrate AI solutions into the existing infrastructure.

Requirements:

  • Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence or related fields.
  • Demonstrable experience in natural language processing and machine learning.
  • Advanced programming skills in languages such as Python, R, and Java.
  • Experience in developing AI solutions in virtual environments.
  • Knowledge of deep learning frameworks such as Tensorflow, PyTorch, or Keras.
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to work in a team.
  • Ability to quickly learn new technologies and processes.

We Offer:

  • Competitive salary based on the candidate's experience and skills (30-50k€).
  • Opportunity to work in a growing company with a dynamic and passionate team.
  • Career growth plan and personalized development opportunities.
  • Creative and innovative work environment.

If you meet the above requirements and you like the idea of working with us in Madrid, please send your updated resume and a cover letter summarizing your relevant experience and motivation for applying. We look forward to hearing from you!
